Why Retrofitting a building is important?

A soft story building is a wood structure building which has a weaker first floor and is unable to carry the stories above during an earthquake. The first floor generally would have large openings in the permiter walls such as garages tucked under the parking or large windows. Retrofit is a improvement to your building by altering or adding structural elements. StructureTech provides retrofitting done to allow our clients a peace of mind in the quality of work.
